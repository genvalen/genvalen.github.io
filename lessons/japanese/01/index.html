Listen for these phrases

  • とうさん、いちどでいいから　あいたかったな
    tousan, ichido de iikara aitakatta na
    --- dad, I want to meet you all at once

  • プレゼントがあるのとうさんからよ
    purezento ga aru no tousan kara yo
    --- there is a present from dad

  • とうさん を いきかえら せる ことがで きるわ
    tousan o ikikaera zeru kotogade kiruwa
    --- dad is coming back

  • まほう が はんぶん しかかかってない
    mahou ga hanfun shikakakattenai
    --- the magic is terrible

  • ふっかつ の じゅもん かんせい させるんだ
    fukkatsu no jumon kansei saseronta
    --- let's make it happen

  • きっと あえる
    kitto aeru
    --- surely you can

  • すこし の まほう と
    sukoshi no mahou to
    --- with a little magic

  • ゆうき が あれば -
    yu kiga areba
    --- if there is a dream

Memorize these vocabulary words

  • おとうさん - otousan --- formal "father" or someone else's father
  • とうさん - tousan --- informal "father" or your own father
  • プレゼント - purezento --- present; gift
  • いきかえる - ikikaeru --- revive; be restored to life
  • あした - ashita --- tomorrow
  • まほう - mahou --- magic; sorcery; witchcraft
  • ふっかつ - fukkatsu --- ressurection
  • やって みれ よ - yatte mire yo --- try it out
  • ゆめ - yume --- dream
  • せかい - sekai --- world

Note - お ( 'o' ) sometimes functions as an honorific. Words prefixed with お contribute a feeling of politeness or respect to the word.
